PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

1. OVERVIEW

This privacy policy describes how this personal Oura application ("App") collects, uses, and protects your health and wellness data. This App is developed and operated solely by Julia Brickey for personal use only.

2. PERSONAL USE APPLICATION

This App is a personal development project and is NOT intended for public distribution or commercial use. The App is used exclusively by the developer to access and analyze their own Oura Ring data.

3. INFORMATION COLLECTED

Through the Oura API, this App may access the following data types:

  • Email address
  • Personal information
  • Daily activity and readiness scores
  • Heart rate data
  • Sleep data and tags
  • Workout information
  • Session data
  • Blood oxygen (SpO2) measurements
  • Ring configuration settings
  • Stress measurements
  • Heart health metrics

4. HOW DATA IS USED

All data accessed through this App is used solely for:

  • Personal health and wellness tracking
  • Data analysis and visualization for personal insights
  • Integration with personal productivity and wellness systems

No data is ever:

  • Shared with third parties
  • Sold or monetized
  • Used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Stored on external servers (data may be stored locally on the developer's devices only)

5. DATA SECURITY

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect accessed data, including:

  • Secure API authentication using OAuth 2.0
  • Local storage on password-protected devices only
  • No transmission of data to external parties

6. DATA RETENTION

Data accessed through this App may be retained locally for as long as needed for personal analysis purposes. The developer may delete any stored data at any time.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

As this is a personal use application accessing only the developer's own data, standard data subject rights apply through your Oura account settings. You may:

  • Revoke API access at any time through the Oura app
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Export your data directly from Oura

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
